Types of gender-based violences

Violence is often associated only with physical violence, neglecting other nonphysical forms. Violence is a complex issue and categorising different types of violence can never be exact.

Sexual Violence

  • Rape and sexual assault
  • Sexual harassment
  • Forced prostitution and trafficking
  • Revenge pornography

Psychological / Emotional Abuse

  • Intimidation and threats
  • Gaslighting and manipulation
  • Stalking and cyberstalking
  • Controlling behavior

Physical Violence

  • Domestic violence
  • Honor killings
  • Physical assault

Financial Abuse

  • Controlling access to financial resources
  • Preventing someone from working or earning money
  • Withholding money or basic needs
