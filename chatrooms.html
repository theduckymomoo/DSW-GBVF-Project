Ukhuseleko: Protection Through Connection

A culturally-sensitive digital platform offering anonymous support and counseling services tailored to the South African context.

Our Features

Anonymous Support

Access completely anonymous chat rooms where your privacy is our priority. No personal information required.

South African Counselorst

Connect with trained counselors who understand local contexts, cultures, and challenges unique to South Africa.

Multilingual Support

Services available in multiple South African languages including English, Xhosa, Zulu, Afrikaans, and more.

Low Data Usage

Services available in multiple South African languages including English, Xhosa, Zulu, Afrikaans, and more.

How it works

1

Sign Up Anonymously

Create an anonymous profile with a nickname. No personal information or email required. Your privacy is guaranteed.

2

Choose Your Support Format

Select between one-on-one counseling or join a moderated group session based on specific topics or challenges.

3

Connect With Counselors

Get matched with a South African counselor who speaks your preferred language and understands your cultural context.

4

Receive Support

Engage in confidential conversations, receive guidance, and access resources tailored to your specific situation.
